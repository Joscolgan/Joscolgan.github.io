New paper on Salmo

June 02, 2021

New paper published in Ecology & Evolution investigating genes and molecular mechanisms underlying alternative migratory tactics in the facultatively anadromous Salmo trutta. We find clear gene differences between migratory and resident morphs of brown trout with distinct transcriptional profiles in the liver but not brain. The project was performed in the lab of Dr. Tom Reed (University College Cork) and led by PhD candidate Rob Wynne.

Wynne et al. Ecology & Evolution, 2021
